Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about HealMate.

HealMate is an online platform that connects patients with trusted healthcare specialists, allowing you to easily search, book, and consult with medical professionals online.

You can book an appointment by searching for a specialist, selecting their profile, and viewing their availability. Once you find a suitable time, you can book directly through HealMate.

HealMate offers both online and in-person consultations, depending on the specialist's availability. You can choose the type of appointment during the booking process.

We have specialists in various fields such as General Practitioners, Dermatologists, Psychologists, Cardiologists, Pediatricians, Endocrinologists, and more.

The cost of consultations varies depending on the specialist and the type of consultation (online or in-person). You can view pricing information on each specialist's profile page.
